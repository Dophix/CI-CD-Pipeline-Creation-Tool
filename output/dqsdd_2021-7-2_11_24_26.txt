
      Create Codecommit Repository :

aws codecommit create-repository --repository-name dqsdd

      Fill Codecommit with .gitignore and README.md files :

aws codecommit create-commit --repository-name dqsdd--branch-name master --put-files filePath=README.md,fileContent='Welcome to our team repository.' filePath=.gitignore,fileContent=''

      Create ECS cluster and service :

aws ecs create-service --cluster cluster/TestClusterVlad--service-name dqsdd

      Create your project pipeline :

aws codepipeline create-pipeline --cli-input-json file://output/dqsdd-template.json
